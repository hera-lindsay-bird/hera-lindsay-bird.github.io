MARGINS, BORDERS & PADDING

The internet is made of boxes.
Any HTML element on a website can be a box.
A picture of a horse. A paragraph of text. A form where users can enter their credit card details. But how do we arrange these boxes on the page? First of all, we have to decide whether or not to use the flexbox or grid model of design. Once we’ve decided on our model and we have some elements we’d like to arrange on a page, this is where understanding borders, padding and margins can be helpful.
